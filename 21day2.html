The Phases

Our gradual approach transitions us slowly into detox mode to reduce hunger and other symptoms and to prepare our digestive systems for the optimal purge.
Whole Food

We start off with 2 days of cooked fruits, veggies, beans and whole grains to get everyone to a common ground so we can move forward together.

Live Food

Enjoy 5 days of nutrient dense raw food for a smooth transition into the liquid phase.

Liquids

Next, we feast on freshly pressed liquids for 7 days With these nutrient dense liquids, our bodies will naturally go into full detox mode while nourishing us to the core.

Water

Somewhere during week 2, we kick the detox up full throttle with water day.

Breaking the Fast

During the 3rd and final week, we will slowly introduce food again, bringing our digestion back up to speed with our new healthier heating habits.
