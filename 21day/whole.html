Whole Food

Fruits, Veggies, Whole Grains, and Beans - Cooked

What does it mean?

Whole foods are unprocessed, or minimally processed, foods that are as close to nature as can be. Food that is “processed” means it has been altered in some way. For example, an apple is whole, while applesauce is an apple that has been processed to a fibrous liquid consistency.

Learning Point: Much of the fiber and nutritional value is removed during food processing. Our bodies get confused with these processed foods, not really knowing how they should be handled. Eventually, the body will equate these unsustainable processed foods to toxins, either storing for later (in the form of fat) or building up a mucus for protection.

What can we eat?

Whole foods consist of fruits, vegetables and tubers from the produce aisle, legumes in the beans aisle, and whole grains in the bulk foods section. Whole foods can be cooked, and we will share with you delicious recipes made of fruits, vegetables, legumes and grains to enjoy during this phase.

  • Fresh fruits (no seedless fruits):
    Citrus, Bananas, Berries, Coconuts, Tropical Fruit, Melons
  • Fresh veggies
    Green Leafy Vegetables, Root Vegetables, Peppers, Mushrooms, Sea Vegetables (Nori, Dulse, Sea Moss, and Arame)
  • Beans
  • Whole grains
    Brown rice, Quinoa, Millet, Farro, Wild Rice, Amaranth
  • Oils
    Grapeseed, Avocado, Extra Virgin Olive, Coconut
  • Dried fruits
    Sun-dried Tomatoes, Figs, Dates, Raisins, Cranberries, Currants - Try to find varieties with no sugar added
  • Seeds
    Sesame, Pumpkin, Sunflower, Hemp
  • Salts And Salty Stuff
    Sea Salt, Crystal Salt, Liquid Aminos, Tamari, Shoyu
  • Sweeteners
    Honey, Maple Syrup, Agave Nectar, And Stevia
  • Herbs and Spices

What should we avoid?

Generally, the category of whole foods excludes meat, chicken and fish; all dairy products, eggs, oils and refined flours and sugars (particularly, bleached).

Specifically, during the whole foods phase, should avoid

  • Animal Protein
    Meat, Fish, Poultry, Eggs
  • Processed Vegetarian Protein
    Tofu, Tempeh, And Other Mock Meats
  • Nuts
  • Dairy
    Milk, Cheese, Butter, Yogurt, Kefir
  • Fast Food
  • All Processed Foods
    Bread, Pasta, White Rice, White Sugar, Table Salt, Canned Foods
  • Artificial Ingredients
  • Artificial Colors
  • Hydrogenated Oils
    Margarine, Crisco, partially hydrogenated oil
  • MSG also know as Monosodium Glutamate
  • Caffeine
    Coffee, teas with caffeine, Soda
  • Drugs and Alcohol
