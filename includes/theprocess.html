Phase 1

First Steps

During this first phase, we will become familiar with the basic foundations of a healthy diet. We will learn the terms and ingredients, as well as how to put develop a menu and grocery shop!

Phase 2

Blast Off

During the first week, we will enjoy nutrient dense whole and raw food for a smooth transition into the liquid phase.

Phase 3

Liquid Level Up

During the 2nd week, we will feast on freshly pressed liquids. With these nutrient dense liquids, our bodies will naturally go into full detox mode while nourishing us to the core.

Phase 4

Live for Five

We will break the liquid phase with raw, living, enzyme rich fruits and veggies that will repair our bodies at the cellular level.

Phase 5

Final Fine Tune

During the final 2 days, we will bring our systems back up to speed with our new healthier heating habits.
